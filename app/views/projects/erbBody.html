The Educational Records Bureau (ERB) developed the Achievement Assessment for Begining Learners (AABL) to replace its desktop assessment for children entering pre-k, kindergarden and first grade.

VGD created the standardized testing platform for iPad with a web-based dashboard for supervisors and administrators. I was the UX lead on the project, while supporting our dev team and project management.

Admin Dashboard

We built a web-app for test administrators to monitor student progress, take notes, and receive alerts if students tapped the “help” button from their iPad.

The admin dashboard is designed to be usable on both tablets and desktop computers.

Teachers are encouraged to take notes on anything that may affect a student’s results.
